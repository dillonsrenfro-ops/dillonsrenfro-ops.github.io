Public Administration

My Career Path

I’m pursuing a Master of Public Administration at Brigham Young University because I believe public service is about helping people and strengthening communities. My goal is to apply data-driven decision making and practical policy design to improve how local governments serve their residents.

Experience in the Field

  • Graduate Assistant – Dean of Students Office, BYU: Conducted program evaluations and analyzed student feedback to improve accessibility and belonging initiatives.
  • Intern – U.S. Senate: Assisted constituents with immigration and passport issues, learning firsthand how government policy impacts individual lives.

Future Contributions

I plan to focus on making government more efficient while preserving the human side of public service. My interests include urban planning, housing affordability, and evidence-based policy development. I hope to teach one day and inspire others to serve with compassion and accountability.
